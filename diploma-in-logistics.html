Diploma In Logistics

This course deals with the management aspects of Logistics like Warehouse and Inventory Management, Management Principles and Practices, Business Communication, E-commerce, Human Resource Management, Logistics and Supply Chain Management, Application of ICT in Supply Chain Management etc. Logistics management is of great importance as this leads to reduced operational costs, improved delivery performance and customer satisfaction.

In the aviation sector, the professionals need to direct the allocation of airport materials, supplies and finished products. They design strategies to reduce the cost and time required to transport goods. They review the present status of logistics functions and identify areas of improvement. They produce aviation performance data and propose improvements to aviation, airport management and customer service. They are responsible for updating with advances in aviation logistics technology and incorporate recent technologies into procedures.

The minimum eligibility for applying for the course is 10+2(in any stream) from a recognized board with 50% of the marks. The duration of the course is 6 months.
