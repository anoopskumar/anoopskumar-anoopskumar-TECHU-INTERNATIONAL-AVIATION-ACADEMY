Who We Are

Techu international aviation, one of the prominent aviation institutions in Kerala provides courses in Airport ground handling, Cabin Crew Training, Hospitality, Diploma in Logistics and Supply Chain Management, Diploma in Airline and Airport Management and Diploma in Airline, Travel and Tourism.

The aviation industry in India has emerged as one of the fastest-growing industries in the country in the past years. India one of the largest domestic aviation markets in the world, there is a massive demand for qualified personnel to work in various sections of the aviation industry.Techu international aviation is one of the topmost aviation academy located at the heart of Kochi.
